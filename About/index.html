  • ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Hello, I am a Howard University graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering. As a STEM major I know it can sometimes feel like it’s an uphill battle learning math especially when you're learning it with other subjects at the same time. I’ve particularly seen the struggles that math students can have picking up a new subject matter - as I’ve dealt with being one. As I progressed in life I began to tutor math and with my years of tutoring the subject I began to see it not just from my perspective but from the perspectives of students from all different walks of life. Through my years of practice, I’ve picked up on the minute details that can make or break students when trying to study the subject at maximum capacity with no lag to the learning process.

Any subject can be treacherous with the wrong teacher, the wrong books, and the wrong resources especially when you’re dealing with a subject that you’re not a natural at it. This can cause you more of a headache and increase the amount of time you need to study. You want to gain more quality studying while speeding up your learning time and that has always been my aim as a math tutor. Whether or not you have working knowledge of the subject at hand I introduce every topic to my students as if they are blank slates and try to make the learning process as visually detailed and interactive as possible – with the addition of theory, giving you the background logic to why the problems operate as they do - so that any and everyone should be able to pick up on how to solve the problems at hand.

I hope this site will be the blessing you are looking for!
