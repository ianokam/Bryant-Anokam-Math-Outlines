SUMMARY

Trigonometry is a word that stems from the Greek words trigōnon (triangle) and metron (measure). As the name implies trigonometry is the study of angles and lengths in relation to triangles, the unit circle (which has a relation to the right triangle), and trigonometric functions (which has a relation to the unit circle). All these ideas are layered on top of each other and as you learn more about the subject the more you'll see the relationship between all the topics. Whether you are studying trigonometry for the first time, want to refresh your memory, or need a little help for a course, this clear, interactive primer will provide you with the skills you need.
