Useful Study Tips

Attention to detail is key to solving your math problems - the less attentive you are to detail the more likely you'll make mistakes and the more time you'll spend on a problem. Here I give a few tips but just know as you read along each of my notes you'll find each topic will be riddled with a some tips to keep you attentive to detail.

  • Always carry a notebook. I find physical books work best. You want to work in the same manner as you would when given any work or a test.

  • As you take notes write EVERY single detail from the problem to the answers. Don't skip a step. - It's tedious but its worth it. Your building that muscle memory !

  • Write your notes in your own words. If you understand the work you are doing you should be able to clearly state it in your own words. If you cannot you should review that topic some more.

  • DONT FOLLOW PATTERNS. You are here to learn the process NOT the pattern to a problem. This is a common mistake some students make and though it may work for you for certain instances you'll find this method of learning will get you into more trouble than anything. DONT DO THIS! try to learn.
